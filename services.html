What We Do

Expert Legal Services

Each practice area is led by specialists who bring deep knowledge and courtroom experience to every matter.

🏛

Civil Litigation & Real Estate

Our civil litigation team handles complex property disputes, land acquisition matters, and real estate transactions across Pakistan. Whether you're dealing with illegal possession, boundary disputes, or title verification issues, we provide strategic representation to protect your property rights.

  • Boundary Disputes
  • Illegal Possession (Qabza)
  • Title Verification
  • Transfer & Registry
  • Stay Order Required
  • Property in Mauza Kahna, DHA, Bahria Town
🏢

Corporate & Commercial Law

We advise businesses — from startups to established corporations — on all aspects of corporate law. Our team handles MOU drafting, SECP registrations, trademark/CCP disputes, and complex commercial litigation, providing both one-time counsel and ongoing corporate retainer services.

  • MOU / Contract Drafting
  • SECP Registration
  • Trademark / CCP Disputes
  • Breach of Contract
  • Commercial Litigation
  • Corporate Retainer Services
📊

Taxation & Wealth Management

Our taxation practice provides comprehensive advisory services for individuals and businesses navigating Pakistan's tax landscape. We offer strategic planning, compliance support, and representation before tax authorities and tribunals.

  • Tax Planning & Strategy
  • Compliance Advisory
  • Wealth Structuring
  • Tax Tribunal Representation
  • Appellate Authority Matters
  • Corporate Tax Advisory
👨‍👩‍👧

Family & Personal Law

We handle all family matters with the highest level of discretion and empathy. Our family law team provides compassionate yet firm representation in inheritance, divorce, custody, and personal status matters — protecting your family's interests at every step.

  • Inheritance (Wirasat)
  • Succession Certificate
  • Divorce / Khula
  • Child Custody
  • General Consultation
  • Family Mediation
⚖️

Criminal Law

Our criminal defense team provides aggressive, strategic representation for individuals facing criminal charges. From pre-arrest bail to trial defense, we protect your rights and freedom with the full force of our legal expertise.

  • Pre / Post-Arrest Bail
  • White-Collar / Corporate Fraud
  • General Criminal Defense
  • FIR Registration / Quashing
  • Police Investigation Matters
  • Appellate Criminal Proceedings
🌍

Overseas Pakistani Services

Our dedicated Overseas Desk serves Pakistanis living in the UK, UAE, USA, Canada, and other countries. We provide end-to-end support for managing legal affairs back home — from remote Power of Attorney to property verification and asset repatriation.

  • Remote Power of Attorney (PoA)
  • Plot Verification & Monitoring
  • Repatriation of Assets
  • NICOP Assistance
  • Property Management
  • Legal Representation in Absentia
